A glimpse of our E-Commerce Logistics benefits

E-commerce logistics are undergoing change and evolution. To reach their customers, retailers all over the world are becoming more robust and swiftly implementing omnichannel, direct-to-consumer (D2C) models. They choose an integrated logistics solutions provider who can connect them with the finest local delivery providers in each location in order to decrease costs, effort, and complexity in their B2C supply chain and allow them to sell online "from factory to sofa" at the same time.

We want to enable brands and retailers to pursue a direct-to-consumer strategy that is fully linked with their current B2B supply chain using RSS E-Commerce Logistics. In order to achieve this, we provide our customers with E-Delivery and E-Fulfillment tools that enable them to sell more items throughout the year while also saving time and money.
