In addition to hundreds of offline trucking points, RSS has a dedicated modern fleet of Boeing and Airbus aircrafts with enormous cargo capacities available to transport loads to more than 74 online international and 56 domestic destinations at present. We have also partnered with numerous trucking companies worldwide to transport cargo safely.

Logistic Management System (LMS)

In order to stay digitally current with industry standards, RSS employs LMS, a state-of-the-art tool for cargo activities that is accessible 24 hours a day, seven days a week. The EDI interface with Indian Customs is supported by the LMS system. The Logistics Management System (LMS) is in operation and offers a full range of services, including booking, acceptance, stowage, unitization, handling, manifestation, carriage, transshipment, delivery, etc., throughout the network.
